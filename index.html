We look forward
to serving you

We are old-world and contemporary. We are casual date
nights and dramatic milestone celebrations. We are
farm-to-table-to-food truck. We are dining in and dining
out, room service and gourmet to go. We are late-night
karaoke, happy hour margaritas, multi-course feasts and
the best burger you ever ate.

We are, at heart, a host of worldwide cuisines, each made intimate and accessible by our head Chef and Founder,
Jose Garces.

Garces Foundation

Through our restaurants and
Luna Farm, we support The Garces
Foundation's mission: to help
Philadelphia’s immigrants, primarily
by funding much-needed healthcare
and providing nutritional programming
for youth.

Luna Farm

Luna Farm is a sustainable,
all-organic 40 - acre farm in Ottsville,
PA. It serves mainly as a family retreat
for the Garces family, and it also
supplies our East Coast restaurants
with the finest locally grown organic
vegetables, fruits, eggs and honey
year-round.
